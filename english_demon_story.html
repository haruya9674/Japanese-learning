The Little Demon Hunter
Long ago, in a quiet village, there lived a small and kind child. The village was peaceful, but deep in the forest, dark demons were waking up. One night, the moon glowed bright red. A tiny demon appeared near the village gate. It was weak, but it scared everyone. The child was afraid, but did not run away. With a brave heart and shaking hands, the child took one step forward. That was the moment the journey began.
